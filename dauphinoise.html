Gratin dauphinois

A French classic, the Dauphinoise potatoes recipe contains thinly-sliced potatoes, cream and (of course) garlic. Simple yet nourishing for the soul on a rainy Sunday afternoon. The most dary cooks will add rosemary, lardons, smoked salmon or cheese on top!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg potatoes
  • 30 cl single cream
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 4 tbsp milk
  • Ground nutmeg
  • Butter (for the tin)
  • Grated cheese (if wanted)

Instructions

  1. Slice the potatoes as thinly as possible.
  2. Half a garlic clove and rub it against the cooking tin. Slice the rest of the garlic in small pieces.
  3. Butter the cooking tin.
  4. Create a first layer of potatoe slices; add salt, pepper, nutmeg and cream. Repeat this step until all potatoes are used.
  5. Pour the milk on the top layer and add cheese if needed.
  6. Place in a pre-heated oven (180Â°C) and bake for 50 min.
