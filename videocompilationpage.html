Climbing techniques

Discover key techniques in three videos to improve your skills and climb like Adam Ondra!

Drop knee

The dropknee is a footwork technique that involves heavily weighting the outside of one foot—with the opposing foot stemmed against another hold—in order to generate body tension.

Dyno

The term “dyno” is a slang abbreviation for “dynamic.” A dyno is a dynamic move, i.e. one in which you use momentum and power to cross a large distance between holds.

Heel hook

The heel hook is a maneuver that elevates your heel above your torso, allowing you to propel your body weight with your leg muscles.
